Do's and Dont's for Mentors

Guide and tips

Do's

What you should do as a good mentor

Be prepared

Be enthusiastic

Treat people with respect

Listen carefully

even when you think you got the question straight away

Promote independence

Explain/discuss so mentee come to answer by himself/herself

Be encouraging but honest

Point on achieved success

Be accessible to the mentee

If possible teach by example

Learn how to look for solution

As it is what devs do most of the time

Be nice ✌️

Dont's

What you should NOT do as a good mentor

Don’t assume people know something

Ask them

Never say - ‘oh, it’s easy!’, ‘you don’t know what is …?’

If person doesn’t understand - don’t repeat yourself

Try to find new words or explanation

Don't expect to bond immediately

It takes time

Don't adopt an authoritative tone

Don’t be a teacher

Be a study-buddy

Don’t be afraid to admit when you are wrong or uncertain

Learning together will strengthen a relationship

Don’t take it personally if a mentee does not heed your advice

Don't take over

Do not do what the mentees should be doing themselves
